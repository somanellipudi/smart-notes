# Suggested Reviewers

**Manuscript**: CalibraTeach: Calibrated Selective Prediction for Real-Time Educational Fact Verification

---

## Suggested Reviewers

### 1. Expert in Calibration & Uncertainty Quantification

**Background**: Researchers specializing in calibration of neural networks, expected calibration error (ECE), and uncertainty quantification in machine learning.

**Rationale**: This work's core contribution is systematic calibration methodology. Reviewers in this area can validate:
- Temperature scaling application and hyperparameter selection (t=1.24)
- ECE computation and statistical significance
- Comparative calibration analysis with baselines
- Multi-metric uncertainty framework (ECE, AUC-AC, confidence intervals)

**Suggested researchers**:
- Authors of seminal calibration papers (Guo et al. 2017, etc.)
- Researchers from calibration-focused labs or groups

---

### 2. Expert in Fact Verification & NLP

**Background**: Researchers specializing in automated fact verification systems, natural language inference, and evidence retrieval.

**Rationale**: Can assess:
- Technical soundness of 7-stage verification pipeline
- Ensemble design choices (6 components and their weights)
- Comparison with FEVER, SciFact baselines
- Transfer learning challenges and domain specificity
- Appropriateness of evaluation metrics for FV

**Suggested researchers**:
- FEVER dataset authors and maintainers
- Fact verification track organizers at ACL/EMNLP
- Researchers publishing in ACL, EMNLP on verification

---

### 3. Expert in Educational AI & Learning Sciences

**Background**: Researchers at intersection of AI and education, intelligent tutoring systems, adaptive learning.

**Rationale**: Can evaluate:
- Education-first system design principles
- Pedagogical feasibility of hybrid human-AI workflows
- Appropriateness of abstention mechanism for classroom
- Validity of pilot study design and limitations
- Alignment with learning sciences principles

**Suggested researchers**:
- ITS community leaders (AIED, Learning @ Scale)
- Educational data mining specialists
- Adaptive learning system developers

---

### 4. Expert in ML Reproducibility

**Background**: Researchers advocating for and operationalizing reproducibility in ML/AI research.

**Rationale**: Can assess:
- Reproducibility protocols (deterministic seeds, cross-GPU validation)
- Code and data availability practices
- Documentation quality for reproduction
- Benchmark dataset quality
- Honest limitations disclosure

**Suggested researchers**:
- ML reproducibility track organizers
- Authors of reproducibility validation papers
- Researchers from institutions with reproducibility focus

---

### 5. (Optional) Expert in Deployment & System Design

**Background**: Researchers with experience deploying ML systems in real-world educational settings.

**Rationale**: Can evaluate:
- Practical feasibility of latency targets (67.68ms)
- Scalability analysis (20,000 claim infrastructure test)
- Cost analysis and GPU efficiency
- Deployment considerations for classroom use
- System reliability and failure modes
