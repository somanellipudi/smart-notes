### Ethical Considerations
The study was designed with the following ethical principles:
1. Transparency: Users informed of AI system capabilities and limitations
2. Human agency: System supports rather than replaces instructor decision-making
3. Fairness: Evaluated performance across domains to detect disparities
4. Accountability: Clear documentation of system limitations
